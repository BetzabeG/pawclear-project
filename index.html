Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are PawClear's simulations? +
Our simulations achieve a 95.7% correlation with real-world data, based on extensive validation against historical research. Each model is continuously improved with new data and peer-reviewed research to ensure maximum accuracy.
Can PawClear completely replace animal testing? +
While our goal is to eliminate the need for animal testing, we recognize that some regulatory frameworks still require limited validation studies. However, PawClear can significantly reduce the number of animals needed by pre-screening formulations and identifying only the most promising candidates for minimal validation.
Is the data from PawClear accepted by regulatory agencies? +
We are actively working with the FDA, EMA, and other regulatory bodies to validate and certify our simulation data. Several agencies now accept our predictive models as supplementary evidence in research applications, and we're making progress toward broader acceptance.
How does the subscription model work? +
PawClear offers tiered subscription plans for academic researchers, biotech startups, and pharmaceutical companies. Each plan includes a set number of simulations per month, access to our AI assistant, and various levels of support. We also offer special pricing for non-profit research organizations.
